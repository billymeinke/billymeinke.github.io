Hi!

My name is Billy and I'm a technologist that works on learning environments.

If you're reading this page, you probably want to know more about me.

I work for the Distance Course Design and Consulting (DCDC) Group in the College of Education at UH Manoa. I've had a hand in many educational technology initiatives over the last several years, focusing on projects that support open, distributed learning. These days I plan and design undergraduate courses and programs, build bicycles, ride waves, and tinker with Web technology that supports learning and creativity.

I facilitate workshops and speak at conferences, and occasionally take on creative projects. If you're interested in getting in touch with me about an opportunity, head over to the contact page.

Blogroll
