Here are click-through examples of work I've done over the last several years.



Design + Dev

I led the development of the CC Toolkits project, a combination of coordinated content sprints and design of a friendly, remixable template for curating support resources about Creative Commons. Site made with Jekyll, Bootstrap, and Github Pages.



Writing

I wrote vignette pieces for CC's Team Open, showing the impact of openness in scientific research and data through stories of people who leveraged openness to do incredible things.






OER Fellowship

In 2014 I was a visiting fellow of the OER Research Hub, collaborating on research and open course development. I wrote several blog posts documenting my experience and sharing the results of a grantee survey about OER adoption in the U.S. DOL TAACCCT program.
